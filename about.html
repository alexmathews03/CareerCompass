About CareerCompass

Hey there! We're a small team of recent grads who remember exactly what it's like to be in your shoes. That feeling of "what's next?" can be super overwhelming, right?

We built CareerCompass to be the fun, simple, and slightly quirky guide we wish we had. No boring corporate jargon, just straight-up ideas and roadmaps to help you figure out your path. We believe that finding a career should be an exciting adventure, not a stressful chore.

Think of us as your friendly neighborhood career pals. We've gathered all this info so you can spend less time stressing and more time exploring what you're passionate about. Let's get started!
